Controls

  • Press Space, S or tap to jump. The longer you press down the higher you'll jump.
  • Press Shift, D or swipe up for a boost. The boost works in the air too, so you can save yourself from the abyss.
  • Press Escape to enter settings/pause the game.
  • Press R to reset the game.
